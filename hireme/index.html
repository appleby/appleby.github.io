Who am I?

I am a software engineer with approximately 7 years of professional experience as a backend & systems software generalist. At my current company, I work on no-code mobile app security. At my previous company, I worked on developer tools for a quantum programming language, including a compiler, simulator, and a python SDK. Prior to that, I worked for a security company on a network scanning appliance that did host, port, OS, application, and vulnerability detection. I have professional experience programming in C/C++, Python, and Common Lisp.

Where am I?
