Who am I?

I am a software engineer with approximately 5 years of professional experience as a backend & systems software generalist. At my previous company, I worked on developer tools for a quantum programming language, including a compiler, simulator, and a python SDK. Prior to that, I worked for a security company on a network scanning appliance that did host, port, OS, application, and vulnerability detection. I have professional experience programming in C/C++, Python, and Common Lisp.

Where am I?

I currently live in Mexico. Ideally, I'd like to find a company that lets me work remotely, but I am a U.S. citizen and am willing to relocate for the right job.

What's up with the 5-year resume gap?

The company I worked at was acquired in 2013. I stuck around for ~8 months after the acquisition, then resigned. I left on good terms. During the period December 2013 - May 2019, I was not looking for work.

Why should you hire me?

You should hire me if you are looking for a decent mid-level engineer. You should not hire me if you need a rockstar/ninja/10x engineer.
